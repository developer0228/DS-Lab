Practical 6B: Ring Algorithm
Aim

Implement Ring Algorithm for coordinator election in distributed systems.

Main concept used

In Ring algorithm, all processes are arranged in a logical ring. When coordinator fails, an election message circulates through the ring, and the process with highest ID becomes new coordinator.

1. Ring.java
Purpose

Simulates leader election using ring topology where election message passes through all active processes.
